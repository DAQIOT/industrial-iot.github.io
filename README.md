
# Industrial IoT Solutions for Smart Monitoring & Data Acquisition

We provide Industrial IoT (IIoT) solutions for **energy monitoring, remote equipment monitoring, and industrial data acquisition systems**.

Our focus is helping factories and industrial companies improve **efficiency, visibility, and operational reliability** through real-time data.

---

## 🔧 What We Work On

- Industrial Sensors (Temperature, Pressure, Current, Vibration, CO2, pH)
- Data Acquisition Systems (Modbus / RS485 / Analog / Digital)
- IoT Gateways & Edge Devices
- Remote Monitoring Systems
- Energy Consumption Monitoring & Analysis
- Industrial Equipment Condition Monitoring

---

## 🏭 Application Industries

- Smart Factory / Industry 4.0
- Energy Management Systems

- Steel & Heavy Industry
- Infrastructure Monitoring

## 📡 What We Aim to Solve

- Lack of real-time visibility in industrial processes  
- High energy consumption without monitoring  
- Equipment downtime and failure risks  
- Difficulties in remote monitoring of distributed assets  
- Fragmented industrial data systems
